# Credits

Credits are a free currency used across the network.

You earn Credits from rewards and voting.

## Where to Spend Credits

- Survival: the Credits Shop
- Oneblock: the Credits Shop
- Creative: the Store NPC

## How to Get Credits

- Rewards: see [Rewards](/servers/general/rewards)
- Voting: [worstserverever.com/vote](https://worstserverever.com/vote)

More detailed reward breakdowns will be added soon.
