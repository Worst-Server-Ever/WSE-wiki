# How to Join on Console

This guide is for Minecraft Bedrock players on console.

## Xbox and PlayStation

1. Download the `Bedrock Together` app on your phone.
2. In the app, enter:
   - IP: `play.worstserverever.com`
   - Port: `19132`
3. Press `Start Server` in the app.
4. On your console, open Minecraft and join the LAN server that appears.
