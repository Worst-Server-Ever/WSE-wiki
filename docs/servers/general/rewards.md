# Rewards

This page covers the current reward systems across the network.

## Shared Playtime Rewards

These playtime rewards are the same on Survival, Oneblock, and Creative.

### Repeating Rewards

| Requirement | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| Every 1 hour online | 5 Credits |
| Every 24 hours of playtime | 50 Credits |

### Milestone Rewards

| Total Playtime | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1 hour | 5 Credits |
| 6 hours | 10 Credits |
| 12 hours | 25 Credits |
| 24 hours | 50 Credits |
| 7 days | 100 Credits |
| 30 days | 500 Credits |

## Survival Rewards Menu

Survival players can use `/rewards` to open the rewards menu and view or claim available rewards.

## Survival Daily Reward

Survival also has a daily claim reward with a 14-day streak track.

- It must be claimed each day.
- Missing a day loses your streak.
- After day 14, the rewards rotate back to day 1.

| Day | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | 1 Iron Ingot |
| 2 | 1 Diamond |
| 3 | 1 Emerald |
| 4 | 1 Credit |
| 5 | 1 Diamond |
| 6 | 1 Emerald |
| 7 | 5 Credits |
| 8 | 3 Diamonds |
| 9 | 1 Emerald |
| 10 | 10 Credits |
| 11 | 1 Diamond |
| 12 | 1 Emerald |
| 13 | 15 Credits |
| 14 | 1 Mending Book |

## Survival AFK Rewards

Survival has an AFK reward system at the AFK area.

- You can receive one reward every 5 minutes while standing in the AFK area.

| Chance | Reward Pool |
| --- | --- |
| 60% | Apple, Cooked Beef, or Golden Carrot |
| 25% | Copper Ingot, Iron Ingot, Gold Ingot, Diamond, or Emerald |
| 10% | Golden Apple |
| 4% | 1 Credit |
| 1% | 1 Netherite Ingot |

## Survival Referral Rewards

Survival has a referral system for inviting new players.

- Your friend can use `/referral apply PlayerNAME` to apply your referral.
- Referring one player gives you 10 Credits.
- Referring 4 players gives you 100 Credits.

More reward types can be added here as they are documented.
